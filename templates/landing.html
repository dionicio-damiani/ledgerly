Everything you need to know

An invoice is a professional billing document sent from a service provider to a client. It details the work performed, quantities, unit prices, applicable taxes, and the total amount due. A proper invoice establishes a clear payment obligation and serves as a legal record for both parties.

A quote is sent before the work begins — it outlines what you plan to deliver and at what price, giving the client a chance to approve before committing. An invoice is sent after the work is done, requesting payment. Ledgerly lets you generate both from the same form.
